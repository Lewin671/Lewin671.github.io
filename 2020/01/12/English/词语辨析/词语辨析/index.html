词语辨析

Posted on 2020-01-12 Edited on 2020-04-27 In English

  1. skirt和dress的区别.

    skirt，dress都有裙子的意思，有什么区别呢？

    dress是连衣裙，包含skirt和上衣部分

    skirt表示短裙.

  2. Mr.和Sir的区别

    Mr.是Mister的缩写. Mr.和Sir的区别如下

    • 称呼的对象不同: 在Mr.后面只能跟姓,表示是熟悉的人,而Sir则表示是:不熟悉或是对上级头头们的称呼。
    • 用法不同: Mr之后可接姓或姓名,如我们可以说Mr Green或Mr Jim Green,但不说Sir Green或Sir Jim Green.sir, 而Sir一般单独使用,不跟姓名连用.

  3. explict和implict的区别.

    用词根分析 -plicit是区分开 的意思 这就是为什么两个词都叫清晰的,无疑问的.

    explicit ex-向外 外部 例如external 说明这种清晰是外在明确的

    implicit im-向内 里面 有一个同源imply 动词意思是含蓄 暗示

    所以,它们的翻译应该为:

    explicit： 明显的，显式的，可以看到的。

    implicit：隐式的，存在但是你没有感觉到的。

  4. elder, older和elderly的区别

    elder, older这两个词都是old的比较级，在用法上有所区别。erlder本身也是形容词。

    • elder表示"前辈的“、“年纪较长的”，仅仅用于同一家庭成员的比较; older则不限于此。比如:

      My younger son is five and my elder son is nine.

      Tom is two years older than I.

    • elder在句子中只做定语，不做表语; older两者皆可.

      My elder sister does her homework all by herself.

      His elder son got married last week.

      I think his mother is older.

    • elder只能用来指人，older既可以修饰人，也可以修饰物.

      This dog is older than any dogs here.

      I have a sister older than myself.

      It is said Mr. Chen is over ten years older than Mrs. Li.

    • elderly是形容词，表示"较老的“。 elderly是委婉用语，意思为上了年纪的。一般不说old man，elderly man代替, the elderly泛指老人.

      His mother’s elder sister is an elderly woman now.

  5. shirt和blouse的区别

    “A blouse is a loose-fitting upper garment that was formerly worn by workmen, peasants, artists, women and children. It is typically gathered at the waist (by a waistband or belt) so that it hangs loosely (“blouses”) over the wearer’s body.” Good and specific definition from the thorough article at http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blouse

    A shirt is the general term for anything from a halter top to a hairshirt, and a blouse refers to something more specific. It doesn’t have to be short or long sleeved. They have buttons or zippers, some kind of closure, and are made of a fine fabric like silk or satin so it can hang loosely.

    blouse一般翻译为女衬衫.

  6. Terminology versus Term

    ‘Term’ refers to a single word. Whereas ‘terminology’ refers to any words that relate to a certain thing. E.g. ‘Abdominal’ and ‘Pectoral’ are medical terms. They are both part of medical terminology.

  7. critic, critical, criticism, criticize, critique的区别

    • critic, 名词，批评家，评论家，爱挑剔的人
    • critical, 形容词，批评的，爱挑剔的，评论的
    • criticism, 名词，批评
    • criticize, 动词， 批评，评论
    • critique, 可以为动词和名词，动词时意思为批判，评论，名词时为批评.

  8. 注意否定句下and和or的用法。

    Jan isn’t rich and happy, which means Jan isn’t rich, or Jan isn’t happy.

    Jan isn’t rich or happy, wich means Jan is neither rich nor happy.
