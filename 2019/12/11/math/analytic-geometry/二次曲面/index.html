{x+z=λ(1+y)λ(x+z)=1−y\begin{cases} x+z = \lambda(1+y) \\ \lambda(x+z) = 1-y \end{cases} {x+z=λ(1+y)λ(x+z)=1−y​

{x−λy+z−λ=0λx+y+λz−1=0\begin{cases} x - \lambda y + z - \lambda = 0 \\ \lambda x + y + \lambda z - 1 = 0 \end{cases} {x−λy+z−λ=0λx+y+λz−1=0​
