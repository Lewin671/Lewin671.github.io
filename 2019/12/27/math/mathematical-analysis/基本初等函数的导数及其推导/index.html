基本初等函数的导数及其推导

Posted on 2019-12-27 Edited on 2020-05-20 In Math , 数学分析

初等函数: 数学分析将基本初等函数归为六类：幂函数、指数函数、对数函数、三角函数、反三角函数、常数函数.

常数函数的导数

假设常数函数为f(x)=cf(x) = cf(x)=c，那么它的导函数dydx=0\frac{dy}{dx} = 0dxdy​=0.

证明:

dydx=lim⁡Δx→0f(x+Δx)−f(x)Δx=lim⁡Δx→0c−cΔx=0\frac{dy}{dx} = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(x+\Delta x) - f(x)}{\Delta x} = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0}\frac{c - c}{\Delta x} = 0 dxdy​=Δx→0lim​Δxf(x+Δx)−f(x)​=Δx→0lim​Δxc−c​=0

对数函数的导数

设对数函数为y=log⁡axy = \log_{a}xy=loga​x，那么它的导数为dydx=1(ln⁡a)x\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1}{(\ln a) x}dxdy​=(lna)x1​.

证明:

dydx=lim⁡Δx→0f(x+Δx)−f(x)Δx=lim⁡Δx→0log⁡a(x+Δx)−log⁡a(x)Δx=lim⁡Δx→0log⁡a(1+Δxx)Δx=lim⁡Δx→01ΔxΔxxlog⁡a(1+Δxx)xΔx=1xlim⁡Δx→0log⁡a(1+Δxx)xΔx=1xlog⁡ae=1(ln⁡a)x\begin{aligned} \frac{dy}{dx} &= \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(x+\Delta x) - f(x)}{\Delta x} \\ & = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0}\frac{\log_{a}(x + \Delta x) - \log_{a}(x)}{\Delta x} \\ & = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{\log_{a}(1 + \frac{\Delta x}{x})}{\Delta x}\\ & = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{1}{\Delta x} \frac{\Delta x}{x} \log_{a}(1 + \frac{\Delta x}{x})^{\frac{x}{\Delta x}} \\ & = \frac{1}{x}\lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \log_{a}(1 + \frac{\Delta x}{x})^{\frac{x}{\Delta x}} \\ & = \frac{1}{x} \log_{a}e \\ & = \frac{1}{(\ln a)x} \end{aligned} dxdy​​=Δx→0lim​Δxf(x+Δx)−f(x)​=Δx→0lim​Δxloga​(x+Δx)−loga​(x)​=Δx→0lim​Δxloga​(1+xΔx​)​=Δx→0lim​Δx1​xΔx​loga​(1+xΔx​)Δxx​=x1​Δx→0lim​loga​(1+xΔx​)Δxx​=x1​loga​e=(lna)x1​​

特别地, (ln⁡x)′=1x(\ln x)' = \frac{1}{x}(lnx)′=x1​.

幂函数的导数

假设幂函数为f(x)=xaf(x) = x^{a}f(x)=xa，那么它的导函数为axa−1a x^{a-1}axa−1

证明:

f(x)=xa=ealn⁡xf(x) = x^{a} = e^{a \ln x} f(x)=xa=ealnx

dydx=ealn⁡x(aln⁡x)′=ealn⁡xax=axa−1\begin{aligned} \frac{dy}{dx} & = e^{a \ln x} (a \ln x)' \\ & = e^{a \ln x} \frac{a}{x} \\ & = a x^{a-1} \end{aligned} dxdy​​=ealnx(alnx)′=ealnxxa​=axa−1​

三角函数的导数

  1. 假设f(x)=sin⁡xf(x) = \sin xf(x)=sinx，那么它的导数为dydx=cos⁡x\frac{dy}{dx} = \cos xdxdy​=cosx.

    证明:

    dydx=lim⁡Δx→0f(x+Δx)−f(x)Δx=lim⁡Δx→0sin⁡(x+Δx)−sin⁡(x)Δx=lim⁡Δx→0sin⁡(Δx)cos⁡(x+Δx/2)Δx=cos⁡x\begin{aligned} \frac{dy}{dx} &= \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(x+\Delta x) - f(x)}{\Delta x} \\ & = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{\sin(x + \Delta x) - \sin(x)}{\Delta x} \\ & = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{\sin(\Delta x) \cos(x + \Delta x /2)}{\Delta x} \\ & = \cos x \end{aligned} dxdy​​=Δx→0lim​Δxf(x+Δx)−f(x)​=Δx→0lim​Δxsin(x+Δx)−sin(x)​=Δx→0lim​Δxsin(Δx)cos(x+Δx/2)​=cosx​

  2. 设f(x)=cos⁡xf(x) = \cos xf(x)=cosx，那么它的导函数为dydx=−sin⁡x\frac{dy}{dx} = - \sin xdxdy​=−sinx

    证明:

    f(x)=cos⁡x=sin⁡(x+π/2)f(x) = \cos x = \sin (x + \pi / 2)f(x)=cosx=sin(x+π/2)，所以根据复合函数的求导法则，有

    dydx=(sin⁡(x+π/2))′=cos⁡(x+π/2)⋅(x+π/2)′=−sin⁡x\frac{dy}{dx} = (\sin (x + \pi / 2))' = \cos (x + \pi / 2) \cdot (x + \pi / 2)' = - \sin x dxdy​=(sin(x+π/2))′=cos(x+π/2)⋅(x+π/2)′=−sinx

  3. 设f(x)=tan⁡xf(x) = \tan xf(x)=tanx，那么它的导函数为dydx=sec⁡2x\frac{dy}{dx} = \sec^{2} xdxdy​=sec2x

    证明:

    f(x)=tan⁡x=sin⁡xcos⁡xf(x) = \tan x = \frac{\sin x}{\cos x} f(x)=tanx=cosxsinx​

    根据求导法则的四则运算，有

    dydx=(sin⁡xcos⁡x)′=(sin⁡x)′cos⁡x−sin⁡x(cos⁡x)′(cos⁡x)2=1(cos⁡x)2=sec⁡2x\begin{aligned} \frac{dy}{dx} & = (\frac{\sin x}{\cos x})' \\ & = \frac{(\sin x)' \cos x - \sin x (\cos x)'}{(\cos x)^{2}} \\ & = \frac{1}{(\cos x)^{2}} \\ & = \sec ^{2} x \end{aligned} dxdy​​=(cosxsinx​)′=(cosx)2(sinx)′cosx−sinx(cosx)′​=(cosx)21​=sec2x​

  4. 设f(x)=sec⁡xf(x) = \sec xf(x)=secx，则f′(x)=sec⁡xtan⁡xf'(x) = \sec x \tan xf′(x)=secxtanx.

    证明:

    dydx=(sec⁡x)′=(1cos⁡x)′=(1)′cos⁡x−1⋅(cos⁡x)′(cos⁡x)2=sin⁡xcos⁡2x=sec⁡xtan⁡x\begin{aligned} \frac{dy}{dx} & = (\sec x)' = (\frac{1}{\cos x} )' \\ & = \frac{(1)' \cos x - 1\cdot (\cos x)'}{(\cos x)^{2}} \\ & = \frac{\sin x}{\cos^{2} x} \\ & = \sec x \tan x \end{aligned} dxdy​​=(secx)′=(cosx1​)′=(cosx)2(1)′cosx−1⋅(cosx)′​=cos2xsinx​=secxtanx​

  5. 设f(x)=csc⁡xf(x) = \csc xf(x)=cscx，则f′(x)=−csc⁡xcot⁡xf'(x) = -\csc x \cot xf′(x)=−cscxcotx

    证:

    f′(x)=(csc⁡x)′=1sin⁡x=−(sin⁡x)′(sin⁡x)2=−cos⁡xsin⁡2x=−csc⁡xcot⁡x\begin{aligned} f'(x) & = (\csc x)' = \frac{1}{\sin x} \\ & = \frac{-(\sin x)'}{(\sin x)^{2}} \\ & = \frac{-\cos x}{\sin^{2}x} \\ & = -\csc x \cot x \end{aligned} f′(x)​=(cscx)′=sinx1​=(sinx)2−(sinx)′​=sin2x−cosx​=−cscxcotx​

  6. 设f(x)=cot⁡xf(x) = \cot xf(x)=cotx，则f′(x)=−csc⁡2xf'(x) = -\csc ^{2}xf′(x)=−csc2x.

    证明:

    f′(x)=(1tan⁡x)′=−(tan⁡x)′(tan⁡2x)=−sec⁡2xtan⁡2x=−1sin⁡2x=−csc⁡2x\begin{aligned} f'(x) &= (\frac{1}{\tan x})' \\ & = \frac{-(\tan x)'}{(\tan^{2}x)} \\ & = \frac{-\sec^{2} x}{\tan^{2} x} \\ & = -\frac{1}{\sin^{2} x} \\ & = -\csc^{2} x \end{aligned} f′(x)​=(tanx1​)′=(tan2x)−(tanx)′​=tan2x−sec2x​=−sin2x1​=−csc2x​

反三角函数的导数

  1. 设f(x)=arcsin⁡xf(x) = \arcsin xf(x)=arcsinx，那么它的导数为dydx=11−x2\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - x^{2}}}dxdy​=1−x2​1​

    证明:

    f(x)f(x)f(x)的反函数为x=sin⁡yx = \sin yx=siny，所以根据反函数的求导法则，有

    dydx=1(sin⁡y)′=1cos⁡y=11−sin⁡2y=11−x2\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1}{(\sin y)'} = \frac{1}{\cos y} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - \sin ^{2} y}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - x^{2}}} dxdy​=(siny)′1​=cosy1​=1−sin2y​1​=1−x2​1​

    注意，这里的(sin⁡y)′(\sin y)'(siny)′是对yyy求导.

  2. 设f(x)=arccos⁡xf(x) = \arccos xf(x)=arccosx，那么它的导函数为dydx=−11−x2\frac{dy}{dx} = - \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - x^{2}}}dxdy​=−1−x2​1​

    证明:

    f(x)f(x)f(x)的反函数为x=cos⁡yx = \cos yx=cosy，所以

    dydx=1(cos⁡y)′=−1sin⁡y=−11−cos⁡2y=−11−x2\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1}{(\cos y)'} = - \frac{1}{\sin y} = -\frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - \cos ^{2}y}} = - \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - x^{2}}} dxdy​=(cosy)′1​=−siny1​=−1−cos2y​1​=−1−x2​1​

  3. 设f(x)=arctan⁡xf(x) = \arctan xf(x)=arctanx，那么它的反函数为dydx=11+x2\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1}{1 + x^{2}}dxdy​=1+x21​

    证明:

    dydx=1(tan⁡y)′=1sec⁡2y=1tan⁡2y+1=1x2+1\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1}{(\tan y)'} = \frac{1}{\sec^{2} y} = \frac{1}{\tan^{2} y + 1} = \frac{1}{x^{2} + 1} dxdy​=(tany)′1​=sec2y1​=tan2y+11​=x2+11​

  4. 设f(x)=arccotxf(x) = arccot xf(x)=arccotx，那么它的反函数为f′(x)=−11+x2f'(x) = - \frac{1}{1+x^{2}}f′(x)=−1+x21​.
